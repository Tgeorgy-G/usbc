About us


USBC is a structure that was founded in the year 2019 to help address the high unemployment rate in the country and amongst graduates.The University of South Africa being the biggest ODL university with about plus 300 000 students, Our aim as the USBC is to build the University Micro economy through our 3 main pillars fist being to identify relevant market opportunities for students in our network, second we provide entrepreneurial training for students that join our network, third we provide support and opportunities to students that already own business.


Busisness opportunities

We conduct extensive market research and identify high growth business opportunities that exist in the university economy for our entrepreneurs and businesses in our network.

Business Support

We provide relevant exposure for businesses that in the USBC network and we assist existing business with excess to the university economy through our campus distribution channels.

Entraprenueral Development

For all the student that are looking to get into entrepreneurship, we offer workshops that will help them develop their entrepreneurial skills. through the market research that will be done by USBC the students will then be given insight on possible market opportunities that the can explore in the university economy
